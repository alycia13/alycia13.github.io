    • Winter Fishing Packages


    All packages include cabin stay, heated fishing house, transportation to and from fish house, bait, and meal plans (additonal rates apply for meal plans). Meal plans include 3 meals per day, with dinner the day of arrival and breakfast the day of departures. Rates shown are cost per person. Weekday rates (Monday - Thursday) are discounted at 15% off original package price. Each cabin sleeps up to 6 people (2 double beds, 2 single beds) with 1 bathroom, full kitchen, living room, fireplace and outside deck. All packages require a $200 deposit per person, with full balance due upon check-in. Deposits are non-refundable, but can the transfered during the same season. Children under 16 are 50% off!
