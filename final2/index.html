    • About North Star Fishing


    Located on world famous Lake of the Woods, Minnesota, we offer premiere fishing to anglers from all parts of the world. Owned and operated by a homegrown Lake of the Woods native, we guarantee your experince to be a personal one that will be remembered for a lifetime. We offer both summer and winter packages, and cozy cabins that give you an at home feel while surrounded by the beauty of northern Minnesota. We also rent fishing boats, jet skis, and a houseboat to further better your experience. Get ready to reel in a good time on Lake of the Woods!
